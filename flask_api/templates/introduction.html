Introduction

Welcome to StoreCast, a predictive analytics tool designed to transform the way Walmart approaches retail forecasting. As one of the largest retailers in the world, Walmart's ability to anticipate customer demand on a weekly basis is critical for making accurate decisions around inventory, promotions, and workforce management. This app leverages time-series modeling to predict future sales, allowing Walmart teams to better align their operational strategies with evolving customer needs and market trends.

Retail operations are highly dynamic, especially when spanning different regions and departments. From seasonal shifts and local festivities to economic influences and promotional events, Walmart stores experience diverse sales patterns. This app uses Auto ARIMA and XGBoost models in an ensemble approach to learn from historical sales trends and predict future store-level performance. By addressing the complexity of these trends, our solution helps Walmart reduce waste, avoid stockouts, and meet customer demand efficiently across all its store locations.

The forecasting model incorporated into StoreCast uses weekly sales data for 45 Walmart stores. This data captures both general trends and anomalies driven by holidays or local variations in demand. Through structured cleaning and preprocessing, we ensure the model is trained on relevant and high-quality inputs.
